CPU


A Central Processing Unit(CPU),also reffered to as cenral processor,is the hardware within a computer that carries out instructions of a computer program by performing the basic arithmetical,logical and input/outpt operations of a system.The term has been inuse in the industry atleast since the early 1960's .The form ,design and implementation of CPU have changed over course of history,but their fundamental operation remains much the same.


ALU


 In computing ,arithmetic and logical unit (ALU)is a digital circuit that performs integer arithmetic and logical operations.The ALU is a central procesing unit of a computer and even is simple microprocessors contain one for such as maintaining timers.
